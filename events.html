About ALU

Alu provides higher education for a higher purpose. Our students
declare missions, not majors.

They develop the real-world skills to take on the world's most
pressing challenges. And they take ownership of their learning from
day one through our peer and student-led-approach, because ALU
believes in the power and agency of young peaople to start shaping
the future right now.

Together with a world-class faculty and staff, our students are igniting
a ripple of positive impact across Africa and the world.
